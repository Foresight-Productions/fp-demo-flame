What We're Firing Up

From Our Oven to Your Table

Everything is made fresh, every day — from dough to sauce, we don't cut corners and we don't use a microwave.

Wood-Fired Pizza

Our 10" personal and 16" large pizzas are fired in a real wood-burning oven for that authentic char, crisp crust, and smoky depth you can't fake.

Crispy Wings

Tossed in your choice of sauce, our wings come out golden and never soggy. The perfect starter — or the whole meal, no judgment.

Oven-Baked Sandwiches

6-inch subs loaded with quality ingredients and finished in the oven for a perfectly melted, toasty result every time.

Fresh Salads

Crisp, fresh greens with house-made dressings. Great on their own, even better alongside a slice or two.

Starters & Desserts

From shareable appetizers to sweet finishes — round out your meal with house favorites that keep people coming back.

Pickup & Delivery

Order online for fast pickup at 302 W Nob Hill Blvd, or get it delivered right to your door. Lunch specials Mon–Thurs, 11am–2pm.
